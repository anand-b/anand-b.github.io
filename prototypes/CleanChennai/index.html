Clean Chennai is a non-profit initiative to make the Chennai city clean. We have often wondered how foreign countries keep their environment clean and hygienic. We also wished our city be transformed to one such place. Clean Chennai initiative supports this cause.
The initiative is at its nascent stage. It requires volunteers from different walks of life and different parts of chennai city. Not just volunteers, it requires aid and cooperation from public to make this initiative a success.
